EE 5G Plus Launch Boosts Network Speeds for 50 Million UK Customers

By OfficeandWin Tech Desk · 2026-04-28

⚡ Quick Summary

  • EE launches 5G Plus with spectrum reallocation across thousands of UK cell sites
  • The upgrade targets indoor coverage gaps and peak-time congestion that have plagued UK mobile users
  • Major cities are first in line for the connectivity upgrade with rollout continuing through 2026

EE, the UK largest mobile network operator, has launched its 5G Plus service with a massive spectrum reallocation campaign across thousands of cell sites. The upgrade promises significantly faster speeds and better indoor coverage for approximately 50 million customers across the United Kingdom.

The Spectrum Reallocation Strategy

Rather than simply adding new spectrum, EE is taking the more efficient approach of reallocating its existing spectrum holdings across its nationwide cell site network. By shifting more spectrum to 5G frequencies and optimizing how 4G and 5G share available bandwidth, EE can deliver faster 5G speeds without waiting for new spectrum auctions or regulatory approvals.

Tackling the Indoor Coverage Problem

One of the most persistent complaints about UK mobile networks has been poor indoor coverage, particularly in dense urban areas where building materials block higher-frequency signals. EE 5G Plus specifically addresses this by allocating more lower-frequency spectrum to 5G, which penetrates buildings more effectively. This should deliver usable 5G speeds inside offices, homes, and commercial buildings where customers previously struggled to get a reliable signal.

Peak-Time Performance

The other major target is peak-time congestion. During evening hours when millions of users stream video and browse simultaneously, UK networks have historically struggled with speed degradation. EE 5G Plus increases available 5G capacity at high-traffic sites, which should reduce the severity of peak-time slowdowns.

Rollout Timeline

The upgrade is rolling out city by city, with major urban areas first in line. EE has not published a complete city list, but customers can check the EE website or app to see if their area has been upgraded. The full nationwide rollout is expected to continue through 2026, with rural areas receiving upgrades later in the cycle.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is EE 5G Plus?

EE 5G Plus is an upgraded 5G service that reallocates spectrum across thousands of cell sites to deliver faster speeds, better indoor coverage, and reduced peak-time congestion.

Do I need a new phone for 5G Plus?

You need a 5G-capable phone, but most modern 5G handsets should work with 5G Plus without any changes. Check your device settings to ensure 5G is enabled.

When will 5G Plus reach my area?

EE is rolling out city by city through 2026. Check the EE website or app for availability in your specific area.
