CNET and TechRadar Report Major Tech Deals as Spring 2026 Upgrade Season Begins

By OfficeandWin Tech Desk · 2026-04-12

⚡ Quick Summary

  • Spring 2026 is bringing significant discounts on laptops, tablets, and software as manufacturers clear inventory for next-generation models
  • Windows 11 laptops with Snapdragon X Elite processors are seeing price drops of 15-25%, making this an opportune time for budget-conscious upgraders
  • Microsoft Surface devices are among the most heavily discounted, with Surface Pro 10 models available at historic lows
  • Buyers should verify Windows 11 compatibility requirements before purchasing any discounted device to avoid activation or compatibility issues

The spring 2026 tech deal season is officially underway, with major retailers and manufacturers offering substantial discounts on laptops, tablets, and software as they prepare shelf space for next-generation hardware expected in the fall. Both CNET and TechRadar have published comprehensive deal roundups highlighting the best current offers, and several patterns are emerging that savvy buyers should understand.

Windows 11 laptops powered by Snapdragon X Elite processors are seeing the most aggressive price cuts. Devices that launched at $999 to $1,299 in late 2025 are now available between $749 and $999, representing discounts of 15 to 25 percent. The price drops reflect both seasonal inventory clearance and competitive pressure from Intel's newly released Lunar Lake refresh chips, which have reset performance expectations for the thin-and-light category.

Microsoft's own Surface lineup is particularly well-positioned for deals this season. The Surface Pro 10, which debuted in early 2025, is now available at its lowest price point since launch. With the Surface Pro 11 having recently shipped with the Snapdragon X Elite, retailers are aggressively discounting the previous generation to move remaining stock. For users who do not need the absolute latest processor, the Surface Pro 10 remains a highly capable machine running full Windows 11 Pro.

Beyond hardware, software deals are also proliferating. Microsoft 365 personal subscriptions are being bundled with laptop purchases at major retailers, and standalone Office 2024 licenses are available at significant discounts from authorized resellers. For users who prefer a one-time purchase over a recurring subscription, this is one of the best windows in recent memory to acquire a genuine Office 2024 Professional Plus license.

Buyers should exercise some caution when evaluating deals. Not every discount represents genuine savings — some retailers inflate list prices before applying percentage-off promotions, a practice known as reference pricing. Check price history tools like CamelCamelCamel for Amazon products or Honey's price tracking for broader retail coverage before committing to a purchase.

Additionally, verify that any discounted laptop meets Windows 11's hardware requirements, particularly if you plan to upgrade from Windows 10. While most devices sold in 2025-2026 meet these requirements, some deep-discount models with older processors may not be eligible for Windows 11 updates, which could create security and compatibility issues going forward.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is spring 2026 a good time to buy a laptop?

Yes. Seasonal clearance discounts combined with new processor launches are driving prices down 15-25% on many models, particularly Windows 11 Snapdragon X Elite laptops.

Should I buy a Surface Pro 10 or wait for Pro 11?

If you need a device now, the Pro 10 at its discounted price offers excellent value. If you want the latest processor and can pay full price, the Pro 11 is the better performer.

Are software deals included in spring sales?

Yes. Microsoft 365 subscriptions are frequently bundled with hardware purchases, and standalone Office 2024 licenses are available at notable discounts from authorized resellers.

How can I tell if a deal is genuinely good?

Use price tracking tools like CamelCamelCamel or Honey to check historical pricing. Be wary of inflated reference prices that make discounts appear larger than they are.

What should I check before buying a discounted laptop?

Verify the device meets Windows 11 hardware requirements (TPM 2.0, Secure Boot), check the processor generation, and confirm the RAM and storage meet your needs.

Are refurbished devices worth considering?

Manufacturer-refurbished laptops often offer 30-40% savings with warranty coverage. They can be excellent value if purchased from reputable sources with return policies.
